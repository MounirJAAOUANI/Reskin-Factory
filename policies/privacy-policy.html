Introduction

Welcome to MoneyMind. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using MoneyMind, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information collected automatically when you use our app:

  • Usage data: App interactions, features used, and session information
  • Device data: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Performance data: Crash reports and error logs to improve app stability
  • Advertising data: Advertising ID for personalized ads (with consent)

Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (GAID)
  • Device identifiers
  • App usage data for ad targeting

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Google Settings → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization). AdMob's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We implement the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) for GDPR consent management in the EU/EEA.

Analytics (Firebase)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our app. Firebase may collect:

  • App events and screen views
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Session duration and engagement metrics

This data is anonymized and aggregated. Firebase privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

You can disable analytics collection in app settings.

In-App Purchases

Premium features are available via Google Play in-app purchases. We do NOT store your payment information — all transactions are processed securely by Google Play.

Purchase records are retained for customer support and refund processing.

Push Notifications

With your permission, we may send push notifications. You can disable them at any time in your device settings or within the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve MoneyMind
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • To understand usage patterns and improve user experience
  • To display relevant advertisements (with consent where required)

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Google AdMob — for advertising
  • Google Firebase — for analytics and app infrastructure
  • Crash reporting services — for app stability

All third-party services are bound by their own privacy policies and applicable data protection laws.

Data Retention

We retain usage data for a maximum of 90 days. Aggregated, anonymized analytics may be retained longer for trend analysis. You can request deletion at any time (see Your Rights section).
